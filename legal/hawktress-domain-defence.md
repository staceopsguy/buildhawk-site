## Why now

The trade mark filing locks the legal right to the name. Domains and handles lock the public-facing surface. If a squatter registers hawktress.com or @hawktress on Instagram between filing and launch, recovery is slow (UDRP for domains, weeks-to-months for social platforms) and costs more than registering today.
